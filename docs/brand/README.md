## Logo Concept

The Monetra logo combines two recognizable elements:

- The letter **M**, representing Monetra.
- A wallet, representing personal finance and transaction management.

The flat master mark is designed to remain recognizable at small sizes and to
serve as the source for monochrome, favicon, horizontal, vertical, light, and
dark logo variants.

## Review Checklist

- [ ] The mark is recognizable at 32 × 32 pixels.
- [ ] The letter M remains visually distinct.
- [ ] The wallet remains recognizable without fine detail.
- [ ] The mark works on both light and dark backgrounds.
- [ ] The final geometry is approved before variants are created.
- [ ] Horizontal and vertical lockups use consistent spacing.
- [ ] Light and dark variants meet contrast requirements.
- [ ] Favicon remains recognizable at 16 × 16 and 32 × 32 pixels.

## Monochrome Usage

The monochrome mark uses a single solid color on a transparent background. Do
not add gradients, shadows, outlines, or multiple colors to this variant.

Use the monochrome mark when:

- Color reproduction is unavailable or unreliable.
- The logo is used as a watermark or embossing reference.
- A simplified, high-contrast mark is required.
